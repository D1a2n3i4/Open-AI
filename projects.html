GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer)

GPT is a family of language models developed by OpenAI. These models are capable of generating human-like text based on the input they receive. GPT has been used for various applications, including text completion, language translation, and text summarization.

DALL-E

DALL-E is an AI model developed by OpenAI that generates images from textual descriptions. Given a textual input, DALL-E can create highly realistic and diverse images that match the description provided. This model has applications in creative design, image generation, and visual storytelling.

CODA

CODA is a research project at OpenAI focused on developing reinforcement learning agents capable of solving complex control tasks in simulated environments. These agents learn to perform tasks such as robotic manipulation, navigation, and game playing through trial and error.
