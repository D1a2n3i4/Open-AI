About OpenAI

OpenAI is an artificial intelligence research laboratory consisting of the for-profit corporation OpenAI LP and its parent company, the non-profit OpenAI Inc. The mission of OpenAI is to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity.

Founded in December 2015 by Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, Wojciech Zaremba, and John Schulman, OpenAI has rapidly grown into one of the leading institutions in AI research.

OpenAI conducts cutting-edge research in various domains of artificial intelligence, including natural language processing, reinforcement learning, robotics, and more. It aims to develop AI systems that are not only highly capable but also safe and aligned with human values.

Through its research, publications, and collaborations, OpenAI strives to advance the field of AI in a responsible and ethical manner, while also promoting transparency and open access to its findings.
